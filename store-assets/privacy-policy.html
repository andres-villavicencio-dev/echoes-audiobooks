Introduction

Echoes Audiobooks ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Echoes Audiobooks mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information Automatically Collected

When you use our App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Playback Progress: We store your listening progress locally on your device and sync it with our servers to allow you to resume playback across sessions.
  • Device Information: Basic device identifiers for analytics and crash reporting.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App (e.g., which audiobooks you listen to, playback duration).

Information Collected by Third Parties

Our App uses third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifiers
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region)
  • App usage data

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Supabase

We use Supabase for backend services. Data stored includes:

  • Playback progress (audiobook ID, chapter, position)
  • No personal identifying information is required

For more information, see Supabase Privacy Policy.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Save and sync your listening progress
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Improve and optimize the App
  • Monitor usage and detect technical issues

Data Storage and Security

  • Playback progress is stored locally on your device and synced to secure cloud servers
  • We do not require account creation or personal information
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data

Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The audiobook content is designed for general audiences. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising (via device settings)
